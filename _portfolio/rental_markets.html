Problem Statement

This report/dashboard serves as a guide for a foreign investor looking to invest into the US rental market, particularly multiple-unit properties (duplex, triplex, etc.).

Deliverable

The report pages include State-to-State Migration and Housing Analysis. The investor can use these pages to determine which states and metro areas to invest in based on migration trends and housing metrics.
